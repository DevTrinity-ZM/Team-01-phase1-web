Student Dashboard

How It Works

  1. Create your Educonnect account and set up your profile.
  2. Join or create study groups for your courses.
  3. Add your classes and deadlines to the timetable manager.
  4. Use academic chat and your dashboard to stay on top of your studies.

About Educonnect

Educonnect is a web-based platform that combines academic collaboration with student life management, helping you organize your classes, connect with peers, and keep track of everything in one place.
